Who We Are

A Church for
Everyone

Located in Ha-Kutama Tshikwarani, Limpopo, Victory Fellowship Christian Centre is a community built on faith, love, and the Word of God.

We are part of the Apostolic Faith Mission of South Africa — united by the belief that every person deserves access to the grace of God.

Our Vision

A church accessible to all people, celebrating our unity and God-given diversity, empowering members for caring and changing communities to the glory of God.

Our Mission

Rooted in John 3:16, we are a missional church making a Christ-centred difference where we live, work and function daily.

Our Values

Faith. Unity. Service. Spirit-led worship and community transformation carried by the love of Christ.
